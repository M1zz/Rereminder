Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

Rereminder ("the App") is developed by Hyunho Lee. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The App operates entirely on your device. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login is required.
  • No personal information (name, email, phone number) is collected.
  • No usage analytics or tracking tools are integrated.
  • No data is sent to external servers.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device to provide its functionality:

  • Timer settings: Duration, pre-alert offsets, and custom messages.
  • Timer templates: Saved timer configurations for quick access.
  • Timer history: Records of completed timer sessions.
  • App preferences: Theme color, sound/vibration mode, and other settings.
  • Purchase status: Whether you have purchased Rereminder Pro (stored in Keychain).

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Apple Watch & Widget

Timer state is shared between your iPhone, Apple Watch, and widgets using Apple's App Group and WatchConnectivity frameworks. This communication happens directly between your own devices and does not pass through any external server.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time "Rereminder Pro" purchase through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Notifications

The App uses local notifications to alert you about timer events. These notifications are scheduled on your device and do not involve any remote push notification service.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal data from any user, the App is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at leeo@kakao.com.
